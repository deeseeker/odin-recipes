Description

Secret weapon? The grill. This recipe makes quick work of potato salad prep. You'll cook the taters whole on the grill. Then, simply quarter them. It's a German-style potato salad with an apple cider vinegar and olive oil dressing. Zero mayo. "Absolutely perfect for summertime grilling," says bd.weld.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds red potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil

Directions

  • Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  • Place potatoes in a bowl; add 2 tablespoons olive oil and toss to coat.
  • Cook on preheated grill until tender, about 30 minutes. Cool potatoes, 10 to 15 minutes; cut into quarters.
